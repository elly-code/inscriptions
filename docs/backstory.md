# Backstory

Initially created by Stella from Team Constellation (https://github.com/teamcons)

I saw an old unmaintained translation app, worked on RollIt who had a Gtk.Paned, and wondered about doing a translation app with a simple UI based on it.

summer 2025 came around a working prototype, but the name and identity couldn't be decided up until 2026. Until then it had the placeholder "Mr Worldwide". But i didnt like that people would think of the rapper Pitbull.

Inscriptions finally came along. It sounded like an app that helps you decipher "inscriptions" on the internet.
@wpkelso (https://github.com/wpkelso/) also made a really cool icon, funded by @t3rminus (https://github.com/t3rminus)

the hieroglyphics was either "beer" or "Seshat", the latter in honour of the ancient egyptian goddess of writing and language. Wpkelso decided on the latter
